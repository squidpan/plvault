**Job Summary**  
  
Business Consultant role supporting public sector organizations by analyzing, designing, and implementing technology-driven solutions. The position focuses on business process analysis, requirements gathering, and ensuring solution adoption for federal, state, and local government agencies.  
  
**Key Responsibilities**  
  
Business Analysis & Requirements Gathering  

- Engage stakeholders to document business needs
- Conduct interviews, workshops, and process mapping
- Create:

- BRDs
- FRDs
- User stories
- Acceptance criteria

- Ensure alignment between business goals and technical solutions

  
Solution Design & Implementation Support  

- Optimize workflows for government services and citizen engagement
- Collaborate on system integration and cloud platform adoption
- Support automation, reporting, and self-service tools

  
Testing & Quality Assurance  

- Develop and execute test cases and UAT plans
- Facilitate UAT sessions and capture feedback
- Track defects and enhancement requests

  
Stakeholder Management & Training  

- Act as liaison between business, IT, and external partners
- Conduct training sessions and support system adoption

  
  
**Qualifications**  
  
Technical & Functional  

- 10+ years of Business Consultant experience
- 3+ years supporting public sector initiatives
- Experience with digital transformation and enterprise systems
- Knowledge of Agile, Scrum, and Waterfall methodologies

  
Public Sector & Compliance  

- Familiarity with:

- FedRAMP
- FISMA
- HIPAA
- CJIS

- Experience with public sector workflows, compliance models, and citizen engagement

  
Soft Skills  

- Strong communication and stakeholder engagement skills
- Analytical, problem-solving, and organizational capabilities
