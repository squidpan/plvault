# 🧠 What is Bloomberg B-PIPE CBBT?

## ✅ Short answer (interview-ready)

> Bloomberg B-PIPE CBBT provides a consolidated best bid and best offer (BBO) for fixed income instruments by aggregating prices from multiple dealers and trading venues.

---

# 🔍 1. What does CBBT stand for?

👉 **CBBT = Composite Bloomberg Bond Trader**

---

# 📊 2. What kind of data does it provide?

## 🔵 Core data:

### 🟢 Best Bid

- Highest price someone is willing to buy a bond

### 🔴 Best Offer (Ask)

- Lowest price someone is willing to sell

---

## 🟡 Consolidated across sources

👉 It combines:

- Dealer quotes
- Trading venues
- Market makers
