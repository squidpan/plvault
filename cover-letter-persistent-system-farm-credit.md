# Hello

Hi Naveen,

Thank you again for taking the time to speak with me about the Senior Business Analyst opportunity supporting the Farm Credit debt platform.

After our conversation, I spent some time learning about the typical debt issuance lifecycle and thinking through the kinds of business processes and capabilities that are typically required in a greenfield modernization effort. To better understand the domain, I began modeling potential process flows and thinking through the epics, user stories, and acceptance criteria that a Business Analyst would typically develop for a project like this. As someone who enjoys learning new business domains, I found the exercise both challenging and rewarding, and it reinforced my enthusiasm for the opportunity. I enjoy understanding complex businesses by breaking them down into processes and requirements, and that's exactly what drew me to this opportunity.

Although I haven't worked on a debt issuance platform specifically, I believe my experience at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York aligns well with this opportunity. For nearly eight years, I supported a multi-year modernization effort that transformed a mission-critical fixed-income market data platform from a legacy Linux/Oracle client-server environment into a modern AWS-based cloud solution. My role included business analysis, requirements gathering, workflow analysis, testing, release coordination, and production support while working closely with business users, developers, QA, infrastructure, and operations teams.

Having participated in a large-scale modernization effort, I understand that success depends as much on well-defined business processes, requirements, and collaboration as it does on the technology itself.

If you feel it would be helpful, I'd appreciate your sharing this note with your colleagues at **Persistent Systems and Farm Credit** as part of my candidacy. I would welcome the opportunity to contribute to this modernization effort while continuing to learn the business alongside the team.

Thank you again for your time and consideration. I look forward to hearing about the next steps.

Best regards,

**Paul Lyu**
