To thoroughly assess a hardwired system with battery backup, follow these step-by-step procedures:

1. Test for Hardwired (AC) Power

To confirm the unit is actually receiving power from the building's electrical system:

- Locate your unit's specific circuit breaker and turn it off.
- Press the **Test** button. If the alarm still sounds, it is running entirely on its battery backup.
- If you turn the breaker off and the alarm **does not** sound, it means the AC power to that detector is off (or the unit is not properly connected).
