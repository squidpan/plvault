Michael Kiwanuka - Cold Little Heart at Haldern Pop Festival 2016  
  
Brilliant keyboard sound from Steve Pringle at 7.22 onwards for the chorus - adds a superb feel to the song at that point. In fact everyone in this great band has their sound nailed absolutely spot on. Really love the sweet sound of Michael Jablonka's vintage Fender Vibrolux Reverb amp - and his playing of course. These guys have spent time crafting their playing and sounds. This song and all the musicians deserve to be much better known. Can't wait to catch them when Big Boris let's us out in the UK. Keep it up guys - great to see talent, instead of image.
