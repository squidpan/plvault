## About The Position

Neuberger is looking for a high-energy, detail-oriented individual to join us as a Senior Business Analyst in the Client Data Strategy team. This team plays a critical role in providing strategic oversight of client data and reporting for our global client coverage organizations. It leads the development and execution of a multi-year roadmap focused on delivering value across the client data lifecycle, including data architecture, governance, acquisition, quality management, business intelligence, and advanced analytics. The role requires close collaboration with cross-functional teams, including Business Analytics, Client Coverage, Finance, Global Technology, Operations and Business Enablement.

## Requirements

- Bachelor’s Degree in IT, Business Analytics, or a related field of study
- 6+ years’ hands-on experience in managing data and analytics projects within Financial Services / Asset Management
- Experience working with client and product data for US mutual funds, UCITS, ETFs, Alternatives, and other fund vehicles, including fund administrator data, firm/office/financial advisor data, and client coverage models
- Strong analytical, writing, and technical skills
- Proficiency in SQL and Tableau (or comparable BI tools) is required
- Demonstrated ability to break down complex business problems into meaningful components and translate them into actionable business solutions
- Strong oral and written communication skills with the ability to explain technical ideas to business users
- Strong attention to detail, business acumen, and excellent time management and organizational skills
- Team player with strong work ethic and focus on meeting deadlines and objectives
- Ability to work independently and demonstrate critical thinking

## Nice To Haves

- experience with Snowflake, Visual Studio, Claude CoPilot, Python, and Salesforce.com (or another CRM platform) is preferred

## Responsibilities

- Collaborate with stakeholders to identify and analyze business needs, translating them into actionable requirements
- Proactively identify opportunities for process improvements. Perform gap analysis and recommend solutions to improve client data management and business outcomes
- Develop and maintain process automation workflows using Visual Studio / Claude CoPilot and Python to streamline data management and reporting processes
- Maintain Client Analytics Database Model with business driven views and custom data sets for simplified downstream consumption and reporting
- Design, build, and maintain business analytics reports and dashboards using Tableau and other visualization tools
- Conduct in-depth analysis of large volumes of client data to extract actionable insights and support strategic decision-making. Deliver business metrics and insights through reports, presentations, and interactive dashboards
- Document detailed business requirements documents, including workflows, process diagrams, and report mock-ups, ensuring clarity and alignment with stakeholders
- Manage project tasks, timelines, risks, and issues, ensuring timely and successful delivery of objectives
- Partner with IT and business teams to validate solutions through user acceptance testing (UAT) and ensure final deliverables meet both technical and business expectations
- Ensure all requirements align with enterprise data management standards
