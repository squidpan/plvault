**Job description:**

We are looking for a Senior Business Analyst/PM with experience in Front office banking applications. The challenge involves managing continuous stakeholder engagement, providing real-time updates, and ensuring the timely delivery of milestones

**Responsibilities:**

- Manage and oversee JIRA boards to track project progress, issues, and upcoming milestones specific to the ongoing Front Office support.
- Facilitate daily interactions with stakeholders, including senior executives, ensuring transparent communication and alignment.
- Develop and deliver compelling presentations to report on project status, identify risks, and showcase achievements.
- Coordinate effectively with shared services teams to ensure seamless execution of project tasks.
- Proactively identify potential project risks and implement mitigation strategies.
- Manage stakeholder expectations and communicate project updates regularly.
- Support milestone tracking and reporting to ensure adherence to project timelines and objectives.
- Foster a collaborative environment within the project team to achieve the common goal of enhancing Front Office banking operations.

**Preferred Skills:**

- Exceptional communication and presentation skills tailored for engaging stakeholders at all levels.
- Strong stakeholder management skills with a focus on Front Office and banking operations.
- Proven experience in managing JIRA boards and coordinating with cross-functional teams.
- Ability to proactively identify and mitigate project risks.
- Prior experience supporting Front Office banking or financial services projects is highly desirable.
- Demonstrated problem-solving skills and adaptability in a fast-paced environment.
- Capable of managing multiple priorities and working under pressure to meet deadlines.
