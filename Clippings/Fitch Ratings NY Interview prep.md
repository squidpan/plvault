> “I start by identifying key stakeholders and conducting discovery sessions to understand both the business goals and pain points. At the Fed, I worked closely with Product owner and Scrum Master.
> 
> I break requirements into functional and non-functional areas—especially performance, reliability, and data accuracy, which are critical in financial systems.
> 
> I then translate those into structured **user stories with clear acceptance criteria in Jira**, and document supporting details in Confluence.
> 
> I validate requirements with both business and technical teams to ensure feasibility and alignment before development begins.”

# 3. Tell Me About a Project You Managed

> “At the Federal Reserve, I **supported deployment and enhancement of a market data pricing application.**
> 
> I worked with Product owner and scrum master to define requirements, translated them into Jira user stories, and coordinated across development, QA, and DevOps teams during releases.
> 
> I also ensured all deployment steps and health checks were properly tested and documented.
> 
> As a result, we improved deployment reliability and reduced post-release issues.”
