# MRPrice STAR Stories

## Story 1 — Technical Refresh Modernization

### Situation

The Central Bank's market-data pricing platform had accumulated significant custom code, custom workflows, and legacy shell-based processing over many years.

The platform required modernization while remaining operational and supporting critical pricing functions.

### Task

Support a multi-year Technical Refresh initiative intended to:

* reduce custom code
* leverage ACPlus out-of-box functionality
* improve maintainability
* improve operational supportability

### Action

* Participated in Technical Refresh activities across business and technical teams.
* Helped analyze existing workflows and data-processing patterns.
* Supported redesign of legacy shell-based data-load processes.
* Assisted testing and validation of refreshed platform functionality.
* Coordinated with stakeholders during migration activities.

### Result

* Reduced dependence on custom platform components.
* Improved maintainability and supportability.
* Enabled future modernization and cloud-migration initiatives.
* Established a more sustainable operational model.

### Interview Themes

* Modernization
* Business Analysis
* Platform Transformation
* Stakeholder Coordination
* Technical Refresh

## Story 2 — Cloud Migration Preparation

### Situation

The pricing platform distributed market data through Oracle tables and consumer-specific Oracle views.

The organization initiated a cloud-migration roadmap focused on service-oriented architecture and API-based distribution.

### Task

Support migration activities and help ensure that consumer access patterns could evolve from Oracle-based consumption to service-based access.

### Action

* Participated in cloud-migration discussions and planning activities.
* Helped analyze consumer data requirements.
* Supported validation of Oracle-derived data against emerging REST API models.
* Assisted testing and verification activities.
* Worked with technical and business stakeholders during migration planning.

### Result

* Improved confidence in migration readiness.
* Helped establish mappings between Oracle-based distribution and API-based distribution.
* Contributed to modernization planning efforts.

### Interview Themes

* Cloud Migration
* REST APIs
* Data Mapping
* Modernization
* Business Analysis

## Story 3 — Pricing Validation and Data Quality

### Situation

The platform processed pricing information from multiple market-data vendors.

Data-quality issues could occur due to:

* missing ticks
* stale prices
* bid/ask spread exceptions
* unusual price movements

### Task

Support pricing validation processes and assist with investigation of suspect pricing.

### Action

* Reviewed pricing exceptions.
* Supported benchmark-validation workflows.
* Investigated missing vendor contributions.
* Worked with business and technical teams to understand pricing anomalies.
* Assisted operational review processes.

### Result

* Improved confidence in pricing quality.
* Helped identify data-quality issues before downstream consumption.
* Supported reliable pricing distribution.

### Interview Themes

* Data Quality
* Root Cause Analysis
* Production Support
* Market Data
* Problem Solving

## Story 4 — Production Support and Market Data Operations

### Situation

MRPrice operated as a mission-critical pricing platform supporting market-data distribution during the business day.

Operational issues occasionally occurred involving vendor feeds, pricing updates, validation processes, and downstream consumption.

### Task

Provide production-support assistance and help maintain platform reliability.

### Action

* Monitored operational processing.
* Investigated missing ticks and feed-related issues.
* Supported benchmark workflows.
* Coordinated issue investigation across teams.
* Assisted troubleshooting efforts involving Oracle, Linux, shell processes, and pricing data.

### Result

* Supported stable platform operations.
* Helped minimize operational disruption.
* Improved visibility into pricing and feed-related issues.
* Contributed to ongoing operational reliability.

### Interview Themes

* Application Support
* Production Support
* Incident Management
* Market Data Operations
* Troubleshooting
