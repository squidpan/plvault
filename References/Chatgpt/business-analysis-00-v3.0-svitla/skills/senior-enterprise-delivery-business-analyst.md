# Senior Enterprise Delivery Business Analyst

## Summary
- Enterprise delivery-oriented Business Analyst focused on workflow discovery and requirements governance.
- Bridges business stakeholders and distributed engineering teams through rigorous documentation and process clarity.
- Common in fintech, lending, enterprise modernization, and consulting delivery engagements.

## Key Points
- Leads workflow discovery and current-state analysis.
- Produces implementation-ready requirements.
- Maintains requirements traceability and decision governance.
- Coordinates stakeholder signoffs and approvals.
- Supports Agile delivery readiness and distributed engineering execution.
- Strong emphasis on documentation rigor and ambiguity reduction.

- Emphasize operational workflow understanding from NY Fed experience.
- Highlight deployment coordination, stakeholder communication, and dependency management.
- Demonstrate ability to document complex operational workflows clearly.
- Frame release coordination and production support experience as workflow governance experience.

## Real-World Usage

- Enterprise workflow transformation initiatives.
- Distributed offshore delivery coordination.
- Regulated workflow environments with audit and signoff requirements.
