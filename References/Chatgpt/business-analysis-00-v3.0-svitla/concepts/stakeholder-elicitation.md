# Stakeholder Elicitation

## Summary
- Extracts business knowledge from operational stakeholders.
- Clarifies assumptions, dependencies, and exceptions.
- Converts tacit operational knowledge into documented workflows.

## Key Points
- Uses structured questioning techniques.
- Requires active listening and validation.
- Identifies conflicting stakeholder perspectives.
- Documents operational constraints and approvals.

## Interview Insights
- Demonstrate communication experience with operations and DevOps teams.
- Highlight coordination between technical and business teams.

## Real-World Usage
- Workflow discovery sessions.
- Current-state operational analysis.
- Future-state transformation initiatives.
