# Workflow Discovery

## Summary
- Discovers how work is actually performed in operational environments.
- Captures workflows, approvals, handoffs, and edge cases.
- Establishes current-state operational understanding before future-state design.

## Key Points
- Includes stakeholder interviews and walkthroughs.
- Documents inputs, outputs, triggers, and dependencies.
- Captures escalation paths and SLAs.
- Identifies hidden operational assumptions.
- Reduces implementation ambiguity.

## Interview Insights
- Discuss operational handoffs from market-data deployment environments.
- Explain how dependencies and escalation workflows were coordinated.

## Real-World Usage
- Fintech lending workflow modernization.
- Insurance underwriting process analysis.
- Enterprise digital transformation initiatives.
