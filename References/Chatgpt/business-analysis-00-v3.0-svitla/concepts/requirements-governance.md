# Requirements Governance

## Summary
- Controls requirements quality, approval, and traceability.
- Prevents scope ambiguity and uncontrolled change.
- Supports delivery predictability.

## Key Points
- Maintains requirements baselines.
- Supports formal signoff workflows.
- Tracks requirement changes and approvals.
- Ensures engineering-ready acceptance criteria.
- Reduces downstream rework risk.

## Interview Insights
- Discuss change tracking and deployment coordination experience.
- Relate operational readiness reviews to governance discipline.

## Real-World Usage
- Agile delivery organizations.
- Offshore distributed engineering teams.
- Fintech and regulated environments.
