# Incident Escalation Workflow

## Summary
- Defines how production issues are triaged and escalated
- Helps restore business operations quickly
- Coordinates communication across business and IT teams

## Key Points
- Capture symptoms, affected users, and business impact
- Classify severity and priority
- Escalate to appropriate support teams
- Track resolution actions and stakeholder updates
- Confirm recovery and close with lessons learned

## Interview Insights
- Strong incident coordination requires both business context and technical awareness
- Prioritization matters because not all incidents have equal operational impact
- Clear communication reduces confusion during high-pressure support events

## Real-World Usage
- Escalating underwriting workflow failures
- Managing user-impacting application incidents
- Coordinating support during release-related issues
