# Failed Deployment Recovery

## Summary
- Provides a structured response to failed application deployments
- Supports rollback, recovery, and post-release validation
- Helps minimize operational disruption

## Key Points
- Confirm the failure and affected functionality
- Notify stakeholders and support teams
- Decide whether to rollback, hotfix, or pause rollout
- Validate application health after recovery
- Document cause, actions taken, and follow-up items

## Interview Insights
- Deployment recovery requires calm coordination and clear ownership
- A BA/Application Support professional may not execute every technical step but must help coordinate impact, communication, validation, and business readiness
- Runbooks reduce confusion during production events

## Real-World Usage
- Recovering failed PEGA workflow releases
- Coordinating post-deployment validation for underwriting applications
- Managing production readiness after release rollback
