# Insurance Workflow Failure

## Summary
- Workflow failure affects underwriting or business processing
- Requires coordination between business users and technical support teams
- May involve configuration, data, access, or integration issues

## Key Points
- Identify which workflow step failed
- Determine affected users, policies, submissions, or cases
- Check whether the issue followed a recent release or configuration change
- Escalate to the right team with business impact and reproduction steps
- Validate recovery with business users

## Interview Insights
- This usecase tests both business process understanding and support coordination
- Strong candidates explain how they separate symptoms from root cause
- Workflow issues often require both business validation and technical triage

## Real-World Usage
- Underwriting case stuck in approval queue
- PEGA workflow routing failure
- Production issue after low-code configuration release
