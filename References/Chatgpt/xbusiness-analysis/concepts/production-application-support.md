# Production Application Support

## Summary
- Ensures enterprise applications remain stable and usable
- Supports production incidents, releases, and operational readiness
- Bridges business users and technical teams during application issues

## Key Points
- Coordinates triage of production defects and workflow failures
- Validates application behavior after releases
- Communicates issue status to stakeholders
- Works with development, QA, infrastructure, and business users
- Maintains support documentation and operational procedures

## Interview Insights
- Application support often extends beyond ticket handling into ownership of operational outcomes
- Strong candidates understand both business impact and technical dependencies
- Support work requires prioritization, communication, and structured troubleshooting

## Real-World Usage
- Supporting underwriting workflow applications
- Coordinating production validation after deployment
- Investigating user-reported application issues
