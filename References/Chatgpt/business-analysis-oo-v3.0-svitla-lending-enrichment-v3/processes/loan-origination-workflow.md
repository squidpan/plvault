# Loan Origination Workflow

## Workflow Steps
1. Borrower submits application.
2. Borrower onboarding validation begins.
3. Financial documentation is collected.
4. KYC and compliance checks execute.
5. Credit bureau and risk-scoring systems evaluate borrower.
6. Underwriting review occurs.
7. Approval or escalation decisions are routed.
8. Funding approval validation executes.
9. Capital disbursement is triggered.
10. Repayment servicing begins.

## Key Risks
- Missing borrower documentation.
- Fraud and compliance failures.
- Incomplete underwriting assumptions.
- SLA violations.
