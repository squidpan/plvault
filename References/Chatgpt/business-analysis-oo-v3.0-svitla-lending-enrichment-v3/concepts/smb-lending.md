# SMB Lending

## Summary
- Financing ecosystem focused on small and medium-sized businesses.
- Often involves rapid underwriting and operational workflow automation.
- Highly dependent on business financial verification and risk analysis.

## Key Points
- Includes credit evaluation and funding workflows.
- Requires document collection and verification.
- Frequently integrates with accounting systems and credit bureaus.
- Strong operational dependency on workflow orchestration.

## Interview Insights
- Understand operational pain points for SMB borrowers.
- Discuss how workflows reduce approval turnaround time.

## Real-World Usage
- Working-capital financing.
- Revenue-based lending.
- Merchant cash advances.
