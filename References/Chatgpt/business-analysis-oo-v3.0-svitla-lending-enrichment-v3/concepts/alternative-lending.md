# Alternative Lending

## Summary
- Lending model outside traditional banks.
- Often technology-driven and workflow-heavy.
- Focused on faster approvals and streamlined underwriting.

## Key Points
- Uses automated risk scoring and underwriting workflows.
- Frequently targets SMB financing gaps.
- Integrates with credit bureaus and financial-data providers.
- Requires strong workflow governance and exception handling.

## Interview Insights
- Understand how lending decisions move through operational workflows.
- Be able to discuss how a BA documents approvals, escalations, and exceptions.

## Real-World Usage
- SMB financing platforms.
- Merchant cash advance providers.
- Online lending marketplaces.
