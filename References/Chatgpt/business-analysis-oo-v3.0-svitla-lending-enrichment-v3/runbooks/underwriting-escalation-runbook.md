# Underwriting Escalation Runbook

## Purpose
Provide structured escalation handling for underwriting exceptions and unresolved borrower conditions.

## Escalation Triggers
- Missing financial documentation.
- Fraud indicators.
- Compliance mismatches.
- Conflicting borrower information.
- High-risk scoring thresholds.

## Escalation Steps
1. Document unresolved issue.
2. Capture borrower and workflow context.
3. Notify underwriting lead.
4. Route to compliance or risk team if required.
5. Update decision logs.
6. Communicate status to stakeholders.
