# Thank You — AEGIS Senior Business Analyst — 2026-05-18

Dear Anu and Gabriella,

Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today regarding the Senior Business Analyst position at AEGIS Insurance Services. I enjoyed learning more about the team, the underwriting applications built on the Pega platform, and AEGIS’ ongoing modernization initiatives.

Our discussion reinforced my interest in the role, particularly because many of the challenges you described align closely with projects I have supported throughout my career.

I especially appreciated the discussion around application modernization initiatives. At the Federal Reserve Bank of New York (FRBNY), I supported a multi-year effort to modernize a large-scale streaming market data application operating across on-premises and AWS cloud environments. An important early phase of the initiative involved technically refreshing the application to optimize integration capabilities and prepare the platform for cloud migration. This included reducing custom legacy functionality, leveraging more out-of-the-box capabilities of the underlying Enterprise Data Management (EDM) platform, improving integrations, and modernizing workflows and operational processes to support long-term scalability and maintainability.

I also enjoyed discussing my prior insurance industry experience at AIG Property & Casualty PMO, AIG Travel Guard, and EmblemHealth, where I supported enterprise business applications, workflow and process coordination.

The role appears to be an excellent match for my background, particularly my recent experience supporting enterprise application modernization, release coordination, QA/UAT, and cross-functional collaboration in regulated environments. I would welcome the opportunity to contribute to AEGIS’ modernization efforts and support the continued evolution of your underwriting platforms.

Thank you again for your time and consideration. I look forward to hearing from you.

Best regards,  
Paul Lyu
