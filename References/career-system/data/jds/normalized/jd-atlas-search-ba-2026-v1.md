Original Job Description

Our client is a leading financial services organization seeking an experienced Business Analyst/ Product Owner to partner with business and technology teams in driving process improvements, data initiatives, and platform enhancements across critical business functions. This individual will play a key role in optimizing workflows, supporting reporting and analytics initiatives, and delivering scalable solutions that improve operational efficiency.

**Responsibilities:**

- Gather, analyze, and document business requirements from key stakeholders
- Prioritize and manage business initiatives, process improvements, and system enhancements
- Partner with business users and technical teams to deliver scalable solutions
- Manage and optimize CRM reporting, dashboards, workflows, and data quality initiatives
- Develop and maintain operational reporting, analytics, and performance metrics
- Analyze business processes and identify opportunities for automation and efficiency improvements
- Support testing, implementation, and user adoption activities for new initiatives
- Drive cross-functional projects from requirements gathering through delivery

**Qualifications:**

- 3–6 years of experience as a Product Owner, Business Analyst, or similar role
- Experience working within financial services, asset management, investment management, banking, or related industries
- Strong CRM experience, including reporting, dashboard creation, and workflow management
- Advanced Microsoft Excel skills, including pivot tables, complex formulas, and data analysis
- Experience translating business requirements into technical solutions
- Strong stakeholder management, communication, and problem-solving skills
- Proven ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent professional experience
- Exposure to AI-enabled business tools, workflow automation, or analytics platforms
- Experience with Power BI, Tableau, or similar reporting tools
