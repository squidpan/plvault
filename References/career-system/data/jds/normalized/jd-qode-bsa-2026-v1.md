**Position:** Senior Business Systems Analyst  
  
**Location:** Hybrid in New York, NY or Fort Mill, SC  
  
**Type:** Full-Time  
  
**Company Overview  
  
**Incedo is a US-based consulting, data science and technology services firm with over 4000 people helping clients from our six offices across US, Mexico and India. We help our clients achieve competitive advantage through end-to-end digital transformation. Our uniqueness lies in bringing together strong engineering, data science, and design capabilities coupled with deep domain understanding. We combine services and products to maximize business impact for our clients in telecom, Banking, Wealth Management, product engineering and life science& healthcare industries.  
  
**Overview  
  
**We are seeking a Senior Business Systems Analyst with strong analytical capabilities, the ability to work hands‑on with data, and the communication skills to translate business needs into actionable insights. The Senior Business Systems Analyst will partner with business, product, data, and technology teams to translate complex business needs into clear analytical outputs, requirements, and process improvements.  
  
This role is ideal for someone who excels in requirements gathering, stakeholder engagement, and business process analysis, and who brings strong domain experience in wealth management, asset management, or institutional trading. The position also supports portfolio‑level initiatives, requiring the ability to analyze business value, assess dependencies, and contribute to governance and prioritization discussions.  
  
**Key Responsibilities  
  
**
- Business Requirements Gathering & Documentation — Lead structured requirements sessions, translate business needs into functional specifications, and ensure clarity across cross‑functional teams.
- Business Process Analysis — Map current‑state and future‑state workflows, identify gaps, and recommend improvements.
- Stakeholder Engagement — Collaborate with senior business partners, advisors, operations, and technology teams to drive alignment and decision‑making.
- Data Exploration & Insight Development — Work hands‑on with data to explore, cleanse, validate, and derive insights that support business decisions.
- Portfolio & Prioritization Support — Contribute to prioritization discussions by assessing business value, risk, dependencies, and strategic alignment.
- Executive & Operational Reporting — Support the creation of clear, concise reporting for senior stakeholders, including decision‑support materials.
- Cross‑Functional Delivery — Partner with product, engineering, and data teams to ensure requirements are understood, implemented, and validated.
- Governance & Program Support — Participate in governance forums, ensuring documentation, traceability, and alignment across initiatives.
  
**Required Qualifications  
  
**
- 8–10+ years in business analysis or analytics‑driven roles.
- Strong ability to understand business context and translate it into analytical outputs and requirements.
- Hands‑on experience with analytics tools and comfort working with complex datasets.
- Demonstrated ability to “play with data” — explore, cleanse, structure, and validate.
- Excellent communication skills with the ability to present to senior stakeholders.
- Experience working in wealth management, asset management, capital markets or institutional trading domains.
- Strong stakeholder management and cross‑functional collaboration skills.
- Solid understanding of business analysis fundamentals, documentation, and reporting.
  
**Preferred Qualifications  
  
**
- Experience in ALM, Practice Management, or related wealth‑management domains.
- Exposure to portfolio governance, prioritization frameworks, or investment decision‑support.
- Familiarity with business value assessment, benefits realization, or cost‑benefit analysis.
