## Job Summary

We are seeking an experienced **Senior Business Analyst** with 12+ years of experience in gathering business requirements, stakeholder management, process analysis, and delivering enterprise-level technology solutions. The ideal candidate should possess strong analytical, communication, and documentation skills, with experience working in Agile and Waterfall environments.

## Key Responsibilities

- Collaborate with business stakeholders, product owners, and technical teams to gather and analyze business requirements.
- Conduct requirement elicitation sessions, workshops, and interviews.
- Create Business Requirement Documents (BRD), Functional Requirement Documents (FRD), user stories, use cases, and process flow diagrams.
- Translate business requirements into functional specifications for development teams.
- Perform gap analysis, impact analysis, and business process modeling.
- Work closely with QA teams during test planning, UAT, and production validation.
- Facilitate Agile ceremonies including sprint planning, backlog grooming, and retrospectives.
- Manage requirement traceability throughout the project lifecycle.
- Analyze data and prepare reports using SQL, Excel, or BI tools.
- Identify opportunities for process improvement and recommend strategic solutions.
- Coordinate with cross-functional teams to ensure successful project delivery.

## Required Skills

- 12+ years of Business Analysis experience in enterprise applications.
- Strong knowledge of SDLC, Agile Scrum, and Waterfall methodologies.
- Experience creating BRDs, FRDs, User Stories, Use Cases, and Acceptance Criteria.
- Excellent stakeholder management and communication skills.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities.
- Experience with process modeling tools such as Visio or Lucidchart.
- Proficiency in JIRA, Confluence, Azure DevOps, or similar ALM tools.
- Hands-on experience with SQL for data analysis and validation.
- Knowledge of API integrations and system interfaces.
- Experience conducting UAT and supporting production releases.

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in Banking, Financial Services, Healthcare, Insurance, or Retail domains.
- Knowledge of business intelligence and reporting tools (Power BI/Tableau).
- Familiarity with data mapping and ETL processes.
- CBAP, PMI-PBA, or Agile/Scrum certifications are a plus.
- Experience working with distributed teams and enterprise-scale projects.
