About the role of the Business Analyst III  
  
Help redefine how claims work gets done—at scale and with purpose. As a Business Analyst III, you’ll assist in the design and operational delivery of automation and AI-enabled workflow solutions that replace manual, repetitive work with reliable digital decisioning. You’ll partner across Product, Technology, and Configuration to build well-controlled process automations, performance dashboards, and data-driven improvements that shorten cycle times, strengthen accuracy, and elevate the member experience. The Business Analyst III is responsible for redesigning workflows rather than manually supporting inefficiencies; identifying opportunities to automate manual business analyst activities; define high-quality business requirements and acceptance criteria; conduct UAT and support production stabilization; and, operate within Claims Optimization rather than fragmented operational support.  
  
What you’ll do:  
\- Translates ambiguous business needs into solid business and functional requirements for small to large projects with moderate complexity.  
\- Effectively elicits information to create concise, organized Business Requirement Documents (BRD) and Functional Requirement Documents (FRD) using standard, industry recognized tools and best practices.  
\- Manages requirements across the lifecycle of a project to ensure end user satisfaction, business stakeholder buy-in, and scope integrity.  
\- Identifies opportunities and contributes recommendations based on analysis of requirements and future impacts.  
\- Partners with business unit and IT or other departments as needed to ensure successful implementation and ongoing business and / or systems support when appropriate.  
\- Creates detailed Use Cases, User Stories and other documents to illustrate business needs.  
\- Facilitate meetings and Joint Application Development (JAD) sessions in a positive way to drive cross functional stakeholders to reach agreements and effective solutions. Meetings may include leadership.  
\- Conducts research and interviews subject matter experts to create "As-is" and "To-be" process flows.  
\- Apply critical thinking to evaluate cross functional dependencies, including upstream and downstream impacts.  
\- Contributes to enhancements of Business Analysis best practices and suggestions for improvements.  
\- Creates business facing test scenarios, test scripts/cases, and mockups of data necessary to conduct testing.  
\- Creates the traceability between requirements and scenarios to ensure business requirements are met.  
\- May monitor and report on testing activity and results.  
\- Consults with others in the development of appropriate training materials to support the needs of functional areas.  
\- May provide support to other entry and junior level Business Analysts.  
\- May investigate and research system problems and enhancements, working collaboratively with IT and business stakeholders to identify options and provide business and / or technical solutions.  
\- Other duties as assigned.  
  
What you’ll bring:  
\- Bachelor’s degree or four (4) years’ work experience. (Required)  
\- Three (3) years of analytical experience in a technical or business environment, including at least 2 years performing business analysis functions (gathering business requirements, documenting process/data flows, and performing gap analyses). (Required)  
\- Experience scaling automation across multiple workflows or operational areas using repeatable delivery patterns. (Preferred)  
\- Familiarity with claims operations or similar process‑driven domains. (Preferred)  
\- Experience building dashboards and operational reporting using Power BI, Tableau, or equivalent BI tools. (Preferred)  
\- Experience using SQL for data analysis (Preferred)  
\- Fluency using AI tools such as Copilot for drafting requirements, analyzing data, summarizing stakeholder input, or accelerating documentation workflows. (Preferred)  
\- Experience with Lean methodology or Six Sigma. (Preferred)  
\- Experience with Azure DevOps, Visio, or Sharepoint. (Preferred)  
\- Experience in healthcare or other regulated industries. (Preferred)  
\- Experience in a project environment that utilizes Agile or Waterfall methodologies. (Preferred)
