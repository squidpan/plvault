# Resume Tailoring Standard

## Purpose

Resume tailoring converts candidate explainability into role-specific resume guidance.

## Required Sections

Each tailoring artifact must include:

- target company and role
- recommended resume family
- summary direction
- top resume themes
- bullets to emphasize
- bullets to rewrite/add
- keywords to include
- keywords/claims to avoid
- cover letter angle
- interview story focus
- next action

## Rules

- Do not claim direct hands-on experience unless already true.
- Use “ramp-up”, “transferable”, or “adjacent” wording for Workday, GIS, SRE, and AI where appropriate.
- AI may be used as a workflow/productivity story, not as AI engineering unless supported by actual experience.
- Prioritize FRBNY, market data, BA/BSA, application support, release validation, production support, runbooks, Linux, Oracle, AWS-connected services, ServiceNow, UAT, Jira, and stakeholder communication.
