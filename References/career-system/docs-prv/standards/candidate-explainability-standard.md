# Candidate Explainability Standard

## Purpose

Explain why each candidate strategy recommendation exists and how to act on it.

## Required Sections

- Recommendation
- Explanation
- Why This Role Is Attractive
- Resume Focus
- Interview / Story Focus
- Risks / Watch Items
- Application Priority
- Next Action

## Rules

- Do not invent salary, benefits, remote policy, or employer-specific facts.
- Treat AI as a tag/interest unless the role is explicitly AI engineering.
- Tie reasoning to Paul-specific experience: FRBNY, market data, BA/BSA, application support, release validation, incident coordination, runbooks, Linux, Oracle, and AWS-connected services.
