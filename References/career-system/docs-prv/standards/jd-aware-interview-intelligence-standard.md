# JD-Aware Interview Intelligence Standard v0.4.4

## Purpose

JD Intelligence extracts job-specific signals for downstream interview prep, skills generation, resume tailoring, and application packages.

## Required Output Sections

- Extracted Tools
- Extracted Platforms
- Extracted Products
- Extracted Domains
- Extracted Methodologies
- JD-Aware Interview Questions
- Resume Story Mapping

## Design

v0.4.4 is deterministic and conservative. It should not hallucinate tools or product names. It mines known terms from normalized JDs and avoids Teal tracker table contamination.
