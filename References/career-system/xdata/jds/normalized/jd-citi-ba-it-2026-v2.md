## Job Description

Citibank, N.A. seeks an IT Business Senior Analyst for its New York, New York location.

## Duties

Work within Citi’s U.S. Personal Banking Technology space on the Deposit, Savings & Secure Lending team and specifically within the Branch Technology team, which serves as the principal point-of-contact for the Branch Business and ensures the delivery of all Branch Mandatory & Strategic initiatives.

Focus on Business Analysis and System Analysis skillset to deliver programs/projects with key partners including Technology, Operations, Legal, Compliance, Business, as well as external vendors.

Provide input during development and implementation phases, including formulation and definition of systems scope, objectives and necessary system enhancements for complex, high-impact projects.

Formulate and define systems scope and objectives for complex projects and foster communication between business leaders and IT.

Consult with stakeholders to solve system issues and problems through in-depth evaluation of business processes, systems, and industry standards, and recommend solutions.

Support system change processes from requirements through implementation and provide input based on analysis.

Consult with business clients to determine system functional specifications and partner with multiple management teams and other units to meet organizational objectives.

Identify and communicate associated risks and impacts of applying technologies to the current business environment, including consideration of business implications.

Serve as advisor to lower-level analysts.

Act as the subject matter expert to senior stakeholders and other team members.

Appropriately assess risk when business decisions are made, demonstrating consideration for the firm's reputation and safeguarding Citigroup, its clients and assets.

A telecommuting/hybrid work schedule may be permitted within a commutable distance from the worksite, in accordance with Citi policies and protocols.

## Requirements

Requires a Bachelor’s degree, or foreign equivalent, in Electronic Engineering, Computer Engineering or Information Technology, or related field.

Requires 5 years of progressive, post-baccalaureate experience as an IT Business Senior Analyst, Business System Analyst, MIS Analyst, Solution Delivery Consultant, Manager – Financial Services Analytics, Business Analyst, or related position analyzing and supporting IT initiatives and programs for broad-based stakeholders, including business and operations departments.

Minimum of 5 years of experience must include:

* Business process modeling techniques
* Requirements gathering
* Documentation tools
* All phases of Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)
* Agile methodologies
* Broad-based stakeholder management
* Solution design and validation
* Change management
* Testing and quality assurance
* Retail banking business processes and products including loans, savings accounts, and credit cards
* Retail banking regulations
* Microsoft PowerPoint presentations to communicate complex IT solutions and business strategies
* Microsoft Word for documentation, report generation, and technical/business documentation
* Microsoft Visio for designing process flows, IT systems diagrams, and business workflows
* Microsoft SharePoint for team collaboration, document management, and workflow automation
* Microsoft Excel and data visualization with charts and graphs
* Data analysis and interpretation for system performance forecasting, KPI development and monitoring, and risk and impact analysis
* Root cause analysis and issue resolution
* Dashboard design for data visualization and reporting to deliver key business insights and information

Applicants submit resumes at Citi careers. Please reference Job ID #26965164.
