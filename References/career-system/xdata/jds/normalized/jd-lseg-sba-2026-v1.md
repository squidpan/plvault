**Role profile:  
  
****Shape the future of Post Trade at LSEG  
  
**LSEG is expanding its Post Trade capabilities with a new suite of services designed to modernize how firms manage clearing, settlement, and operational processes. As part of this growth, we’re hiring a **Senior Associate** to join our **Equity TradeAgent Product Development** team in New York.  
  
If you’re passionate about building new products, improving industry workflows, and working with OTC Equity Derivatives in an Agile environment, this is an opportunity to make real impact in a global market‑leading organization.  
  
**What You’ll Be Doing  
  
**As a Senior Associate, you’ll play a key role in designing, developing, and delivering new capabilities for LSEG’s expanding Post Trade ecosystem. You will:  
  
- Contribute to the innovation and build‑out of new products and services.
- Translate end‑user needs into clear product requirements and solution specifications.
- Work closely with technology teams to support development, testing, and release cycles.
- Partner with internal stakeholders and external clients to resolve queries and ensure successful adoption.
- Support automated testing and quality assurance across development sprints.
- Identify opportunities to enhance current systems and shape future technology direction.
- Produce high‑quality documentation and contribute to risk and issue management.
You’ll be joining a fast‑moving team with significant visibility and growth potential as our Post Trade services scale.  
  
**What We’re Looking For  
  
**We’re seeking someone who enjoys solving complex problems, thrives in Agile delivery, and understands the realities of post‑trade operations in financial markets.  
  
**Required experience:  
  
**
- Looking for 4–7 years of relevant experience in product development, business analysis, post‑trade operations, or related financial services roles.
- Background as a Business Analyst or Product Owner within a Scrum/Agile team.
- Experience in a delivery‑focused role within a middle‑office, post‑trade, investment bank, institutional investor, or clearing organization.
- Ability to participate in Agile ceremonies, write user stories, define acceptance criteria, and refine backlogs.
- Strong understanding of end‑to‑end processes and dependencies across systems and services.
- Risk and control mindset with hands‑on testing experience.
- Strong analytical skills, problem‑solving ability, and attention to detail.
- Proficiency with tools such as MS Office, Jira, Lucid, Figma, Signavio, Tableau & Alteryx
- Excellent communication, stakeholder management, and collaboration skills.
- Proactive, organized, and comfortable managing shifting priorities.
  
**Ideal experience:  
  
**
- Exposure to JSON, FpML, or FIX message structures.
- Significant knowledge of OTC Equity Derivatives, including an understanding of the trade structure, terms, lifecycle and associated settlement processes. Experience with additional asset classes is advantageous, though secondary to equity product knowledge.
- Awareness of industry challenges across the OTC Equity Derivatives landscape.
