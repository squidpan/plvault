**Overview  
  
**The Enterprise Applications Analyst is responsible for supporting, maintaining, and enhancing enterprise business applications, with a primary focus on **Workday** and other enterprise platforms. This role works closely with developers, business stakeholders, and IT teams to ensure applications are stable, efficient, and aligned with business needs.  
  
This position serves as a functional and technical liaison, contributing to system configuration, analysis, testing, and day-to-day application support while helping maintain the integrity and performance of enterprise systems.  
  
**What We Provide  
  
**
- Referral bonus opportunities
- Generous paid time off (PTO), starting at 30 days of paid time off and 9 company holidays
- Health insurance plan for you and your loved ones, Medical, Dental, Vision, Life and Disability
- Employer-matched retirement saving funds
- Personal and financial wellness programs
- Pre-tax flexible spending accounts (FSAs) for healthcare and dependent care
- Generous tuition reimbursement for qualifying degrees
- Opportunities for professional growth and career advancement
- Internal mobility, generous tuition reimbursement, CEU credits, and advancement opportunities
  
**What You Will Do  
  
**
- Partner with business stakeholders to gather, analyze, and document business requirements across Workday and other enterprise applications.
- Collaborate with developers and technical teams to support system enhancements, integrations, and issue resolution.
- Act as a liaison between business units and IT, ensuring clear translation of requirements into system solutions.
- Provide day-to-day application support, including troubleshooting, issue resolution, and root cause analysis.
- Support multiple enterprise applications (including but not limited to Workday ERP modules such as HCM, Finance, Payroll).
- Monitor system performance and identify opportunities for improvement and optimization.
- Participate in the Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC), including requirements gathering, testing, deployment, and post-production support.
- Develop and execute test plans (unit, integration, and UAT support) to ensure quality delivery.
- Analyze system workflows and recommend improvements to enhance efficiency and user experience.
- Assist in managing inbound tickets and escalations; identify trends and support long-term solutions.
- Create and maintain system documentation, including process flows, configurations, and user guides.
- Support audits, compliance efforts, and data integrity initiatives across enterprise systems.
- Contribute to enterprise application projects and continuous improvement initiatives.
  
**Qualifications  
  
**
- Education: Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, a related discipline, or the equivalent Required
  
**Work Experience  
  
**
- 2–4 years of experience in enterprise applications, systems analysis, or ERP environments.
- Experience supporting Workday or similar enterprise platforms in a functional or technical capacity.
- Exposure to application support, system enhancements, and production issue management.
- Experience working with cross-functional teams in a business and IT environment
- Working knowledge of Workday architecture, modules, or comparable ERP systems.
- Experience with system configuration, reporting, or integrations (Workday experience is a plus).
- Understanding of SDLC methodologies and systems analysis practices.
- Ability to assess system capabilities and support integrations across enterprise platforms.
- Familiarity with ticketing systems and production support environments.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with attention to detail.
- Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment.
- Effective communication skills with both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
- Sound judgment when evaluating and recommending system solutions.
- Collaborative mindset with the ability to work across teams.
- Experience in healthcare or regulated environments.
- Familiarity with Workday releases and updates.
- Exposure to reporting and analytics tools (e.g., Workday reporting, dashboards).
