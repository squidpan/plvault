# Resume Generation Standard

## Purpose

Generate role-specific Markdown resume drafts from resume-tailoring artifacts.

## Required Sections

Each generated resume must include:

- target company and role
- recommended resume family
- professional summary
- core skills
- selected highlights
- professional experience
- additional positioning notes
- claims to avoid / manual review notes

## Safety / Accuracy Rules

- Do not invent employer names, dates, titles, degrees, certifications, or tools.
- Do not claim direct hands-on experience where the tailoring artifact says to avoid it.
- AI should be positioned as AI-assisted analysis/documentation/workflow unless a future source proves production AI delivery.
- Workday, GIS, SRE, and Python should be framed carefully where appropriate.
- Generated drafts are not final resumes; they require manual review before application.
