# Resume Compression Standard

## Purpose

Reduce generated resumes toward a practical two-page target while preserving truthful evidence.

## Rules

- Current or most recent role should normally have no more than 10 bullets.
- Recent relevant roles should normally have 3-5 bullets.
- Older roles should move into `Earlier Experience` or `Earlier Technical Experience`.
- Remove course names that are not actual certifications.
- Avoid duplicate employer sections and duplicate bullets.
- Two pages is a target, not a hard guarantee because Teal formatting may vary.
