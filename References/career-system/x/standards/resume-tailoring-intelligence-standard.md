# Resume Tailoring Intelligence Standard v0.4.6

## Purpose

Resume Tailoring Intelligence converts JD intelligence and gap analysis into explicit resume strategy.

## Required Sections

- JD Signals Used
- Promote These Themes
- De-Emphasize These Themes
- Resume Story Recommendations
- Suggested Resume Strategy

## Design

v0.4.6 is deterministic. It generates tailoring recommendations, not final resume text.
