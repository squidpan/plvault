# Candidate Strategy Standard

The strategy layer converts technical candidate matching into personal pursuit strategy.

Required outputs:
- technical match score
- personal strategy score
- combined score
- pursuit recommendation
- resume strategy
- reasons
- risk flags
- next action

Allowed recommendations:
- pursue_first
- apply_this_week
- apply_selectively
- network_or_research_first
- lower_priority

Do not invent benefits, salary, remote status, or employer facts.
