BR-CS-002
Application Package Quality Improves Over Time

Business Objective
------------------
Career System should become more valuable as more
job descriptions, applications, resumes, gap analyses,
interview preparation packages, and outcomes are accumulated.

Business Rationale
------------------
The system should leverage prior knowledge and evidence
to improve future recommendations and application packages.

Expected Outcomes
-----------------
- Better resume tailoring
- Better gap analysis
- Better interview preparation
- Better role prioritization
- Better application recommendations

Success Indicators
------------------
- Increased interview rate
- Increased offer rate
- Reduced time spent on poor-fit opportunities
- Increased reuse of prior knowledge and artifacts
