# TERM-CS-009 Requirements Traceability

## Definition

Requirements traceability is the ability to follow a business objective through epics, stories, data models, APIs, acceptance criteria, UAT, implementation, and operational use.

## Purpose

Traceability helps prove that implementation work supports a real business need and that requirements have been validated.
