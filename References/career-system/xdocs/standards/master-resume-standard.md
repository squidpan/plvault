# Master Resume Standard

## Purpose

Master resumes are the source-of-truth resumes used by Career System generators.

## Required Sections

Each master resume must contain:

```text
# Paul Lyu — Master <Lane> Resume

Contact block

## Target Positioning
## Professional Summary
## Core Strengths
## Work Experience
## Education
## Publications
## Skills
## Languages
```

## Work Experience Rules

Each job must use this structure:

```text
### Company — Client / Business Context
**Title** | MM/YYYY – MM/YYYY | Location

- Bullet
- Bullet
```

## Rules

- One employer section per role.
- No duplicate employer sections.
- No duplicate bullets inside the same employer section.
- Use bullets only under experience sections.
- Keep master resumes truthful and broad.
- Do not over-tailor master resumes to one JD.
- Tailoring belongs in assembled/final resume versions.
