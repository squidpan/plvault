# Interview Prep Standard v0.4.3

## Purpose

Interview prep files convert JD, role, resume, and gap analysis artifacts into practical interview preparation notes.

## Inputs

- `data/gap-analysis`
- `data/jds/normalized`
- `data/roles`
- `data/resume-versions/teal-export`

## Outputs

- `data/interview-prep/interview-prep-<role>.md`

## Required Sections

- Role Snapshot
- Positioning Statement
- Strengths To Emphasize
- Gaps To Prepare For
- Likely Interview Questions
- Gap-Based Answer Strategy
- Story Bank
- 30-Minute Study Guide
- Questions To Ask Them

## Design Notes

v0.4.3 is deterministic and intentionally conservative. It creates a strong first-pass prep document, not a final interview script.
