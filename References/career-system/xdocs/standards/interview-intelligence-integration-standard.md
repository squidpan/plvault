# Interview Intelligence Integration Standard v0.4.5

## Purpose

Interview prep should combine role/gap-driven questions with JD-aware intelligence.

## Inputs

- `data/gap-analysis`
- `data/jds/normalized`
- `data/roles`
- `data/resume-versions/teal-export`
- `data/jd-intelligence`

## Output

- `data/interview-prep/interview-prep-<role>.md`

## Required Additions

Compared to v0.4.3, v0.4.5 interview prep must include:

- JD Intelligence Summary
- JD-Aware Interview Questions
- JD-to-Resume Story Mapping
- Full Interview Question Set combining JD-specific and role-template questions
