## Job description

ITEC/DEFI/FIN Business Analyst  
The ITEC/DEFI Business Analyst for FIN will be responsible for production support services and project development/implementations. The Business Analyst will report to the ITEC/DEFI/FIN Manager.  
FIN activities consist of financing products – loans, letter of credits, discount loans, and guarantees. Technologies employed to support these activities include MYSIS LOAN IQ and series of applications that act as interfaces to Basel 2 initiative, money transfer, general ledger, money market funding, and risk systems. Additional responsibilities also include supporting existing reports and new reports development.  
The primary responsibility is to gather, analyze, document, and validate the needs of the business stakeholders. The business analyst will use the full range of business analysis skills including: user interface, interviewing, workflow analysis, testing, documentation, gap analysis and specification writing. Production Support is another key responsibility of the Business Analyst.  
Responsibilities include:  
 Elicit business requirements from business users and subject matter experts  
 Analyze and prioritize requirements  
 Define and document requirements  
 Write functional specifications  
 Creation of test plans and test scripts  
 Model the requirements/specifications  
 Perform business process design in conjunction with operations teams  
 Lead requirements validation and traceability analysis  
 Manage scope and requirements throughout the project lifecycle  
 Lead development of common standards and tools  
 Assist in operational rollout and support  
 Define and implement accounting mapping for commercial lending activities  
 Manage base code table changes in Loan IQ.  
Production Support Services for Business related Issues  
Experience performing business analysis activities for large, complex financial software systems, as well as:  
 5-8 years of financial/ IT experiences  
 Experienced with business analysis methodologies, modeling tools and processes and documentation standards  
 Experience in/knowledge of capital market, debt instruments, commercial loan operation  
 Loan IQ with accounting experience preferred  
 Working knowledge of Oracle SQL, basic XML skills  
For the role in working closely with business users and facilitation of communication of requirement, the business analyst should possess:  
 College degree  
 Strong analytic skills  
 Strong written and verbal communication skills to both business users and technical staffs  
 Effective professional relationship building skills  
 Consensus building and facilitation skills  
 Basic project management skills  
 Ability to execute in an independent fashion
