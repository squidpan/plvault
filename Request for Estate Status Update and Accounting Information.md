## Request for Estate Status Update and Accounting Information

Dear Woojin,

We hope you are doing well.

First, we would like to acknowledge and thank you for the time and effort involved in administering Mother and Father's estate. We understand that acting as executor carries significant responsibilities and that there are legal, tax, and administrative matters that must be completed before the estate can be finalized.

Thank you for informing us on April 2nd that probate was granted and that the downtown condominium was back on the market. As beneficiaries of the estate, we would appreciate a status update regarding the administration process since April 2nd and the anticipated timeline for completion.

For example, we see from the link to the listing that the condominium sale was completed on or about April 28, 2026; and we have yet to receive any information on the Estate account. We would thus appreciate information regarding the current status of the estate, including: 

- Status of the downtown condominium sale, including the net proceeds received by the estate.

For convenience and transparency, we are copying the estate counsel, the trustee, and the beneficiaries on this message.  
  
We would appreciate any update you can provide.  
  
Thank you again for your efforts on behalf of the estate.  
  
Sincerely,  
  
Paul, Jinie, Claire
